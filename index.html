About BerylAidCoin

BerylAidCoin is a compassionate and impactful token designed to support those affected by Hurricane Beryl.

Our Objectives

  • Provide direct aid to hurricane victims.
  • Promote community engagement in disaster relief.
  • Ensure transparency and regular updates on fund usage.
  • Build a strong community around a charitable cause.

How It Works

Every transaction of BerylAidCoin incurs a 0.05% tax which is donated to the Community and Disaster Relief Fund.

Token Allocation

  • Founder: 25%
  • Liquidity Pool: 40%
  • Community and Disaster Relief Fund: 30%
  • Development and Marketing: 5%
